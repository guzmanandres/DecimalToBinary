Decimal to Binary Converter

  • Divide the decimal number by 2.
  • Record the remainder. This will be either 0 or 1.
  • Update the decimal number. Take the quotient (the result of the division) as your new number.
  • Repeat steps 1-3 until the quotient is 0.
  • Read the remainders in reverse order. The first remainder will be the least significant bit (rightmost), and the last remainder will be the most significant bit (leftmost).
